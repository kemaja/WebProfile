Pannkakor (Swedish Pancakes)

‚úì Recipe updated!
Cost per serving: ~8 kr
Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 dl1.25 cups flour - 3 kr (Lidl)
  • 6 dl2.5 cups milk - 8 kr (ICA)
  • 33 eggs - 9 kr (Coop)
  • 1 tbsp1 tbsp sugar - 1 kr (any store)
  • 1/2 tsp1/2 tsp salt - 0.50 kr
  • 50g1.8 oz melted butter + extra for frying - 10 kr (Lidl)
Total cost: ~32 kr for 4 servings

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat eggs and gradually add milk.
  3. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Add melted butter and whisk until smooth. Let batter rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Heat a thin layer of butter in a large skillet or pannkakspanna over medium heat.
  6. Pour batter to make thin pancakes, tilting pan to spread evenly.
  7. Cook for 1-2 minutes until bottom is golden, then flip and cook 1 minute more.
  8. Stack finished pancakes on a plate and keep warm.
  9. Serve with lingonberry jam and whipped cream, or sugar and lemon.
