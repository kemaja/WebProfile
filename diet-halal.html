Understanding Halal Food

What is Halal?

Halal means "permissible" in Arabic and refers to food that complies with Islamic dietary laws as outlined in the Quran and Hadith.

Key Requirements

  • No pork or pork products
  • No alcohol or alcohol-derived ingredients
  • Meat must be slaughtered according to Islamic principles
  • No blood products
  • Gelatin must be from halal sources

Common Halal Certifications in Sweden

  • Islamic Society of Sweden - Most common
  • Halal Control - European standard
  • WHF (World Halal Foundation)
  • Local mosque certifications
