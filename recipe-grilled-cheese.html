Instructions

  1. Slice the cheese into 3mm1/8 inch thick pieces.
  2. If using mustard, spread a thin layer on one side of 2 bread slices.
  3. Place cheese evenly on the mustard-covered slices, then top with remaining bread.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and add half the butter.
  5. Place sandwiches in the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Add remaining butter to the pan, flip sandwiches, and cook another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat when both sides are golden and cheese is melted.
  8. Cut diagonally and serve immediately with pickles or lingonberry jam.
